Site Plan for Healthy Living Community

Site Name

Healthy Living Community

This name represents a community-focused platform dedicated to promoting wellness, fitness, and nutrition. It was selected because it reflects the website's mission to inspire healthier lifestyles and connect like-minded individuals.

Site Purpose

The Healthy Living Community website aims to provide a comprehensive resource hub for individuals seeking to improve their well-being through better nutrition, fitness, and wellness practices. The site will offer healthy recipes, fitness guides, wellness tips, and information on local events that promote a balanced lifestyle. It will be a supportive space for users to connect and engage with health-conscious content and activities.

Scenarios

  • What are some easy and nutritious recipes for beginners?
  • Where can I find local events focused on wellness and fitness?

Color Schema

  • Teal (#2a9d8f): Used for headings and buttons to create a calm, welcoming feel.
  • Coral (#e76f51): Used as an accent color for links, buttons, and call-to-action sections to draw attention to important elements.
  • Light Grey (#f5f5f5): Background color for the site to maintain a clean and minimalistic look.
  • Dark Grey (#333): Used for body text to ensure readability against the light background.

Typography

  • Arial: Used for body text to ensure readability and a clean appearance across all devices.
  • Roboto Slab: Applied to headings for a modern, professional look that complements the content's focus on health and wellness.
